Why Called Up Exists

Learn How to Make Disciples.

I started Called Up because I know what it's like to feel like the only Christian on your team. You're trying to live out your faith, but it's hard when there's nobody around you doing the same thing.

This community exists to connect believers who are playing college ball and actually help them grow — not just in their faith, but in how they can pour into the guys around them and make disciples.

You don't have to figure it out alone. There are guys just like you on rosters all over the country. Come find them.
