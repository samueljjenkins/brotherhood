Called Up exists to disciple Jesus followers inside college baseball — and send them back to bring the gospel to their own teams.

Why Called Up Exists

You're Not the Only One Following Jesus.

Called Up started because we kept meeting guys who loved Jesus and loved baseball but had no idea how to connect the two. Guys who were grinding through their season feeling like their faith was something they did on Sunday, not something that had anything to do with what was happening Monday through Saturday.

We create space for baseball players to grow through Scripture, real conversation, and practical training in what it looks like to love God and love your teammates — not just as a player, but as a witness.

This isn't about building something big. It's about seeing one faithful guy per roster go back to his team and let the Holy Spirit do what only He can do.

The Weekly Call

Are You Living on Mission?

Every week we get on a call — open the Word, hear from guys who are further down the road, pray for our teams, and talk honestly about what it looks like to follow Jesus in a college baseball locker room.

The goal is simple: grow in your faith, learn how to lead a Discovery Bible Study, and go back to your team ready to carry the gospel with you.

  • Get discipled by people who understand what you're going through.
  • Get connected with followers of Jesus around the country.
  • Learn how to actually reach your teammates.
